Who am I?

Hi, there!
I am a PhD student working on Marine Microbiology, Viroplogy, Ecology at the Institute for Chemical Research, Kyoto University, in the team of Prof. Hiroyuki Ogata.

Currently, my research topic is the to identify the hosts of NCLDVs, Nucleocytoplasmic Large DNA Viruses. I am working with both "dry" and "wet" methods, and hope could contribute to our knowledge of the micro world.
